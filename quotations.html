Quotations

Send professional quotations to customers and convert them to Sales Orders or Sales Invoices with one click.

Creating a Quotation

1

Go to Selling → Quotation

Click New Quotation.

2

Select the customer

In the Customer field, type and select the customer name. Their address and contact are auto-filled.

3

Set validity date

Set the Valid Till date — this is when the quote expires.

4

Add items

In the Items table, click Add Row. Select each item, quantity, and confirm the rate. VAT is calculated automatically.

5

Submit

Click Submit. The quotation is now locked and can be sent to the customer (PDF button in the toolbar).

Converting to Invoice or Sales Order

Once the customer accepts the quote:

  • Open the Quotation
  • Click the Make button in the top toolbar
  • Choose Sales Order (for orders to be fulfilled later) or Sales Invoice (to invoice immediately)

All items and amounts are copied over automatically.
