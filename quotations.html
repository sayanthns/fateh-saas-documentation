Quotations

Send a price quote to a customer before raising an invoice. When they accept, convert it to an invoice with one click.

Your quotations list

Click Quotations in the left menu to see all your quotes and their status.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Quotations list — all your sent quotes

Creating a new quotation

Click + New Quotation. Fill in the form:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
New Quotation form — select customer, add items, set validity
1

Select the customer

Choose from your existing customers. Don't see them? Add a new one first from the Customers section.

2

Set the date and validity

The Quotation Date is today by default. Set Valid Till to the date the quote expires (e.g., 30 days from now).

3

Add items

Click + Add Item. Select the item, enter the quantity and price. Keep adding rows for each item.

4

VAT is calculated automatically

If Apply VAT (15%) is checked, the total will include VAT. You'll see the subtotal, VAT amount, and grand total at the bottom.

5

Save and send

Click Save as Draft to save, or Submit to finalise. You can then print or email the quotation to the customer.

Converting to an invoice

When the customer agrees to the quote, open it and click Make Invoice. A sales invoice is created with all the items already filled in — no need to enter them again.
