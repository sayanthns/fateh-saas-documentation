Suppliers

Manage your vendor master list — companies or individuals you purchase goods and services from.

Adding a Supplier

1

Go to Buying → Suppliers → New

2

Fill in supplier details

Enter the Supplier Name, Supplier Group (Local, Foreign, etc.), and the VAT Registration Number (for Saudi suppliers).

3

Add bank account (optional)

In the Accounts tab, add bank account details for this supplier to make payment processing easier.

4

Save

Key Fields

FieldDescription
Supplier NameLegal name of the vendor
Supplier GroupCategory for reporting (Local, International, Services, etc.)
Tax IDSupplier's VAT number (for input VAT claims)
Payment TermsDefault payment days (e.g., Net 30)
CurrencyDefault currency for purchase invoices

Supplier Outstanding Balance

Open a Supplier and scroll to the Accounts section to see outstanding amounts — what you still owe to this supplier.
