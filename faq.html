Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions from Fateh ERP users.

Account & Login

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

On the login page, click Forgot Password, enter your email, and follow the link in the reset email. If you don't receive it, check your spam folder or contact support@fateherp.com.

Can multiple users log in at the same time?

Yes. Fateh ERP supports multiple concurrent users. Each user has their own login and can be assigned different roles (e.g., Accounts Manager, Sales User) to restrict what they can see and do.

How do I add a new user?

Go to Settings → Users → New User. Enter the new user's email, assign a role, and click Send Welcome Email. They'll receive login instructions by email.

Invoicing & ZATCA

Can I edit an invoice after submitting it?

No. Once submitted, a Sales Invoice is legally binding and locked. If you made a mistake, you need to issue a Credit Note to reverse the original invoice, then create a corrected invoice.

My ZATCA submission failed. What do I do?

Check the error message on the invoice in the ZATCA section. Common fixes include adding the customer's VAT number for B2B invoices or correcting the address. Then cancel the invoice, fix the customer or item data, and create a new invoice.

Does Fateh ERP support credit notes?

Yes. Open the original Sales Invoice and click Make → Return / Credit Note. Adjust the quantities and amounts as needed and submit.

Can I issue invoices in USD or another currency?

Yes. On the Sales Invoice, change the Currency field. The VAT will still be calculated and reported in SAR as required by ZATCA.

Stock & Items

My item stock is showing negative. How do I fix it?

A negative stock usually means you sold or issued more items than you had. Do a Stock Reconciliation (Stock → Stock Reconciliation) to set the correct quantities.

Can I sell a service without tracking stock?

Yes. Create the item with Maintain Stock = No. It will appear on invoices but won't affect warehouse stock levels.

Data & Backups

How is my data backed up?

Fateh ERP performs automatic daily backups. All data is stored securely in the cloud. You can also download a manual backup anytime from Settings → Backups → Download Latest Backup.

Can I import my existing data from Excel?

Yes. Fateh ERP supports data import for Customers, Suppliers, Items, and more. Go to Settings → Data Import, download the template for the doctype you want, fill it in, and upload.
