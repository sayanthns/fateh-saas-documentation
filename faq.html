Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions.

Login & Account

I forgot my password. How do I reset it?

Click Forgot password? on the login screen, enter your email, and check your inbox for a reset link. If you don't receive it within 5 minutes, check your spam folder or contact support@fateherp.com.

Can I have more than one user on my account?

Yes. Ask your Fateh account manager or email support to add extra users. Each user has their own login credentials.

Can I use Fateh ERP on my phone?

Yes. Open your browser on any phone or tablet and go to your Fateh ERP link. The interface adjusts to fit smaller screens automatically.

Invoices & Payments

Can I edit an invoice after I've submitted it?

No. Once submitted, an invoice is final (this is a ZATCA requirement). If you made a mistake, cancel the invoice and create a new one with the correct details.

A customer overpaid — what do I do?

Record the full amount they paid. The extra amount will show as a credit on their account, which can be applied to their next invoice. Contact support if you need help with this.

How do I issue a refund or credit note?

Open the original invoice and look for the Return / Credit Note option. This creates a credit note that reverses the original invoice (fully or partially).

My invoice shows "Overdue" — what does that mean?

The due date on the invoice has passed and the customer hasn't paid yet. Follow up with the customer. Once they pay, record the payment and the invoice will move to Paid.

Items & Stock

I added an item but can't find it in the invoice dropdown

Make sure the item is saved (not just a draft). Go to Items and confirm it appears in the list. Then try creating the invoice again.

Do I have to use Items? Can I just type a description on the invoice?

You need to select items from your items list. If you have a one-off service, create a generic item like "Professional Services" and change the description and amount each time.

VAT & ZATCA

I don't see a ZATCA status on my invoice

Make sure you clicked Submit (not just Save as Draft). Draft invoices are not sent to ZATCA. Only submitted invoices are cleared.

Do I need to file my VAT return through Fateh?

Fateh ERP records all your VAT data. However, you (or your accountant) still need to log into the ZATCA portal to submit the actual VAT return and pay the amount due each quarter.
