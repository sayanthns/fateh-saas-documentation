Items (Products & Services)

Items are the products and services you buy and sell. Every invoice and quotation is built from items. Create your items library before you start transacting.

You only need to create each item once. After that, it appears in every invoice and quotation dropdown automatically.

Opening the Items list

Go to Stock → Items in the left menu. You'll see all your existing items:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Items list — currently empty, ready for your first item

The table shows each item's name, item code, group, and unit of measure. When you're just getting started the list will be empty.

Creating a new item — step by step

Click the + New Item button at the top right. An empty item form opens:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
New Item form — all fields blank and ready to fill
1

Item Name

Enter a clear, descriptive name. This is what you and your customers will see on invoices. Example: Office Chair - Executive or IT Consulting (per hour).

2

Item Code

A short unique code for this item. It appears on print formats and helps you find items quickly. Example: CHAIR-001, CONSULT-HR. If left blank Fateh will auto-generate one.

3

Item Group

Choose a category: Products for physical goods, Services for consultancy or labour, or create your own group. Groups help in reporting.

4

Unit of Measure (UOM)

How is this item counted? Common choices: Nos (pieces/units), Kg, Litre, Box, Hour, Day.

5

Is Stock Item

Enable this toggle if you physically track inventory levels (e.g., chairs in a warehouse). Leave it off for services — there is no physical stock to track.

6

Standard Selling Rate

Set the default price you charge customers. This pre-fills on invoices but can be overridden per transaction.

Saving the item

Click Create Item (or Save). The item is created and you are taken to its detail page:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Item saved — the detail page showing all item information

From the item detail page you can:

  • Edit any field and click Save
  • View stock ledger — every movement in and out of stock
  • Set multiple selling rates for different price lists
  • Attach files (product images, spec sheets)

Items list after adding items

After adding a few items your list looks like this:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Items list — showing Office Chair, Consulting Service and Laptop after creation

You can use the search bar at the top of the list to filter by name or code.

Item groups & categories

Grouping items makes reports more useful. Typical groups for a trading company:

GroupExample items
ProductsComputers, Furniture, Office Supplies
ServicesConsulting, Installation, Support
SoftwareLicenses, SaaS subscriptions
Raw MaterialsSteel, Fabric, Chemicals (if you manufacture)
VAT tip: If an item is subject to 15% VAT (most goods/services in Saudi Arabia), make sure the correct Item Tax Template is attached to the item. Go to the Taxes tab on the item form and add your VAT template.
