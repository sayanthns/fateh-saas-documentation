Items

Items are the products and services you sell. You need to create an item before you can add it to an invoice or quotation.

Items list

Click Items in the left menu. You'll see all your items with their price and stock level.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Items list — all your products and services

Adding a new item

Click + New Item. Fill in the form:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
New Item form — enter item name, code, price and stock details
1

Item Name

A clear name your team will recognise. Example: Office Chair or Consulting Service (1hr).

2

Item Code

A unique code for this item. This appears on invoices. Example: CHAIR-001.

3

Unit of Measure (UOM)

How this item is measured or sold. Common options: Nos (pieces), Kg, Box, Hour.

4

Rate (selling price)

The price you charge customers for one unit of this item, before VAT.

5

Is Stock Item?

Tick this if you physically track inventory (e.g., goods in a warehouse). Leave unticked for services.

6

Click Create Item

The item is now available to select when creating invoices and quotations.
