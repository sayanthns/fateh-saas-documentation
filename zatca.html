ZATCA e-Invoicing

Fateh ERP is fully compliant with ZATCA Phase 2 (Integration Phase). Every Sales Invoice is automatically signed, cleared, and reported to ZATCA.

Good news: Fateh ERP handles ZATCA compliance automatically. You don't need to understand the technical details — just submit your invoices normally and Fateh does the rest.

What is ZATCA Phase 2?

ZATCA Phase 2 (Integration Phase) requires all VAT-registered businesses in Saudi Arabia to:

  • Generate invoices in a specific XML format (UBL 2.1)
  • Digitally sign every invoice with a cryptographic certificate
  • Submit B2B invoices for clearance in real-time
  • Submit B2C invoices for reporting (within 24 hours)
  • Include a QR code on every invoice

If an Invoice Fails ZATCA Clearance

Fateh shows the error from ZATCA on the invoice. Common reasons:

  • Missing customer VAT number — required for B2B invoices
  • Invalid VAT number format — must be 15 digits
  • Certificate expired — contact Fateh support to renew
  • Duplicate invoice number — each invoice must have a unique sequential number

Fix the issue, cancel the failed invoice, and create a new one.
