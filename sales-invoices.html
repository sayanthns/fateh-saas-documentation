Sales Invoices

Create ZATCA-compliant VAT invoices for your customers. Fateh ERP automatically generates the XML and QR code required by ZATCA Phase 2.

Sales Invoices submitted in Fateh ERP are automatically sent to ZATCA and receive a clearance stamp within seconds.

Creating a Sales Invoice

1

Go to Accounts → Sales Invoice → New

Or from a Sales Order: open the order and click Make → Sales Invoice.

2

Select Customer

Type and select the customer. Their billing address and VAT number will be auto-filled.

3

Set posting date

The Posting Date is the official invoice date. Set it to today or the correct supply date.

4

Add items

In the Items table, add each item with quantity and rate. Select the correct VAT tax template (Standard 15%, Zero-rated, or Exempt).

5

Review totals

Check Net Total, VAT Amount, and Grand Total. The VAT breakdown is shown in the Taxes and Charges table.

6

Submit

Click Submit. Fateh generates the ZATCA XML, signs it, and submits it for clearance. The invoice is now legally binding.

Invoice Types (ZATCA)

TypeWhen to use
Standard Tax Invoice (B2B)Selling to a registered business (with VAT number)
Simplified Tax Invoice (B2C)Selling to an individual consumer
Debit NoteIncrease the amount on a previously issued invoice
Credit NoteReduce the amount (full or partial refund)

Checking ZATCA Status

After submitting, scroll down to the ZATCA section on the invoice. You'll see:

  • Cleared — ZATCA accepted and cleared ✅
  • Reported — Submitted for reporting (simplified invoices) ✅
  • Failed — ZATCA rejected — check the error message and correct the invoice

Printing & Sending

Click the Print button to get a PDF of the invoice. The PDF includes the ZATCA QR code. You can also click Email to send the invoice directly from Fateh ERP.
