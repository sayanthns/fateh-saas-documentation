Sales Invoices

Create invoices for your customers. Fateh ERP automatically calculates VAT and submits the invoice to ZATCA — you just fill in the details and click Save.

Your invoices list

Click Invoices in the left menu. You can filter by All, Draft, Unpaid, Paid, or Overdue.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Invoices list — filter by status to see what's paid and what's outstanding

Creating a new invoice

Click + New Invoice. The invoice form opens:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
New Sales Invoice form — select customer, add items, VAT is auto-calculated
1

Credit Invoice or Cash Invoice?

Choose Credit Invoice if the customer will pay later. Choose Cash Invoice if they're paying right now (cash sale).

2

Select the customer

Pick the customer from the dropdown. If they're not listed, click + to add them on the spot.

3

Set Invoice Date and Due Date

The invoice date is today by default. The due date is when payment is expected (e.g., 30 days later).

4

Add items

In the Items table, select each item, enter the quantity, and confirm the rate. Click + Add Item for each new line.

5

Check VAT and total

Make sure Apply VAT (15%) is ticked if this sale is taxable. The Subtotal, VAT, and Total update automatically.

6

Click Save as Draft or Submit

Save as Draft lets you review before finalising. Submit locks the invoice, sends it to ZATCA, and makes it ready to share with your customer.

ZATCA is handled automatically. When you submit an invoice, Fateh ERP sends it to ZATCA and gets clearance. You don't need to do anything extra — just submit the invoice normally.

Printing or emailing the invoice

After submitting, open the invoice and click Print to download a PDF, or click Email to send it directly to the customer. The PDF includes the ZATCA QR code.
