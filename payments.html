Payments

Record incoming payments from customers and outgoing payments to suppliers.

Receiving a Payment from a Customer

1

Open the Sales Invoice

Go to the paid invoice and click Make → Payment Entry in the toolbar. The payment form opens with the customer and amount pre-filled.

2

Select payment mode

Choose Mode of Payment: Bank Transfer, Cash, Cheque, or Credit Card.

3

Set reference number (optional)

For bank transfers, enter the Reference No. (e.g., bank transaction ID) and Reference Date.

4

Submit

Click Submit. The invoice status changes to Paid.

Partial Payments

If a customer pays part of the invoice, change the Paid Amount to the partial amount before submitting. The invoice will show as Partly Paid. You can create another payment entry for the remainder later.

Advance Payments

To record an advance payment before issuing an invoice:

  • Go to Accounts → Payment Entry → New
  • Set Payment Type to Receive
  • Set Party Type to Customer and select the customer
  • Leave the invoice reference blank
  • Submit

When you later create the invoice, you can apply the advance payment by using Get Outstanding Invoices in the payment entry.
