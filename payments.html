Recording a payment

The easiest way is directly from the invoice:

1

Open the invoice

Go to Invoices, find the invoice that was paid, and click on it to open it.

2

Click "Record Payment"

A payment entry opens with the customer name and amount already filled in.

3

Choose how they paid

Select the payment mode: Bank Transfer, Cash, Cheque, or Credit Card.

4

Add reference (optional)

For bank transfers, enter the bank transaction reference number. This helps you match payments later.

5

Save

Click Save. The invoice status changes to Paid and the customer's outstanding balance decreases.

Partial payment

If a customer pays only part of the invoice, change the Amount to what they actually paid. The invoice will show as Partly Paid. Record another payment when they pay the rest.
