Accounts Receivable

See which customers owe you money, how much, and how overdue their payments are.

Opening the Report

Go to Accounts → Reports → Accounts Receivable. Set the As of Date (usually today) and click Refresh.

Reading the Report

ColumnMeaning
CustomerCustomer name
Invoice No.Sales Invoice number
Invoice DateDate the invoice was issued
Due DateWhen payment is due
Grand TotalTotal invoice amount (inc. VAT)
OutstandingAmount still unpaid
Age (Days)How many days since the invoice date
0–30 / 30–60 / 60–90 / 90+Aging buckets for analysis

Following Up on Overdue Invoices

Filter the report by Overdue invoices to see only unpaid invoices past their due date. Click any invoice to open it and send a reminder from within Fateh ERP (Email button).

Export this report to Excel monthly and share with your management team for cash flow tracking.
