Settings

Configure your company profile, invoice defaults, print templates, POS receipts, and item categories — all from one place.

How to open Settings: Click Settings in the left sidebar under the System section. Only admin users can change settings.

Settings is divided into six tabs. Each tab controls a different part of the system:

TabWhat you configure
CompanyCompany name, VAT no., CR no., contact info, address, logo, and default currency
Invoice & StockWhether invoices update stock, default warehouse, and negative-stock rules
Print TemplatesChoose the printed layout style for invoices, quotations, and orders
POS ReceiptThermal/A4 receipt header, footer, and paper width
CategoriesCreate and manage item categories used in POS
DocsQuick links to this documentation site

Tab 1 — Company

This tab controls the information that appears on every printed document (invoices, quotations, receipts) and in the ZATCA QR code.

Logo & Branding

Upload your company logo to have it appear on all printed invoices and quotations.

1
Click the upload area or drag-and-drop your logo file. Accepted formats: PNG, SVG, or JPEG.
2
Keep the file under 800 KB for fast rendering on printed documents and receipts.
3
Choose Logo Size on Print: Small (40px), Medium (65px), or Large (90px) — this controls how large the logo appears on the printed page.
4
Click Save Settings at the bottom of the page.

Tab 2 — Invoice & Stock

Controls how invoices interact with your stock (inventory) and what happens when stock runs out.

SettingWhat it doesRecommended
Update Stock on Invoice When enabled, saving a Sales Invoice automatically deducts the items from your stock balance. Disable if you handle deliveries separately. Enable for simple retail/service businesses
Default Warehouse The warehouse that items are issued from when you create an invoice. You can override this on individual invoices. Set to your main store/warehouse
Allow Negative Stock When enabled, you can invoice items even if the stock balance would go below zero. Useful for service businesses that track stock loosely. Disable for strict stock control
VAT Account Setup: This tab also has a "Setup VAT Accounts" button. Click it once when you first set up your company — it automatically creates the input and output tax accounts in your chart of accounts. You only need to do this once.

Tab 3 — Print Templates

Choose how your printed invoices, quotations, and sales orders look. Two template styles are available.

TemplateBest forFeatures
Professional 🎨 Client-facing documents, formal invoices Full colour, blue Fateh branding, ZATCA-style tax layout, logo prominently placed
Compact 🖨️ Black & white printing, thermal printers Monospace font, minimal colour, smaller file size, prints faster

You can select a different template for each document type:

  • Sales Invoice — the template used when you print or email a customer invoice
  • Sales Order — the template used for order confirmations
  • Quotation — the template used for price quotes sent to customers
Tip: Click Preview next to any template card to see how a sample document will look before committing to a choice.

Tab 4 — POS Receipt

Customise the thermal or A4 receipt that prints when you complete a Point of Sale transaction.

SettingWhat it does
Receipt Header Text that appears at the top of every POS receipt. Use it for a greeting, shop tagline, or branch name (e.g. "Thank you for shopping at Al Noor Store!").
Receipt Footer Text at the bottom of the receipt. Common use: return policy, Wi-Fi password, or social media handle.
Paper Width 80mm — standard thermal till roll. A4 — for inkjet/laser printers or PDF receipts.
Show VAT Breakdown When enabled, prints a line showing the VAT amount separately (required for tax invoices above SAR 1,000).

Tab 5 — Categories

Item categories are groups you assign to your products and services. They appear as filter buttons on the POS screen, making it faster to find items during a sale.

Create a Category

1
Go to Settings → Categories.
2
Type the category name in the input field — e.g. Electronics, Food & Beverages, Services.
3
Click Add. The new category appears in the list immediately.
4
Go to each item (Items section) and assign it to this category using the Item Group field.

Delete a Category

Click the trash icon next to a category. If items are still assigned to that category, Fateh will warn you and prevent deletion — reassign those items first.

Note: Categories are shared between the POS screen and the Items module. Deleting a category that has items will fail — you must move those items to a different group first.

Tab 6 — Docs

The Docs tab is a quick-launch panel that links directly to this documentation site from within the app.

Each card opens the relevant documentation page in a new browser tab:

  • Getting Started — login walkthrough and initial setup
  • Items & Inventory — adding products, services, and stock
  • Sales & Invoicing — customers, quotations, invoices
  • Purchasing — suppliers and purchase invoices
  • ZATCA e-Invoicing — Phase 2 compliance and QR codes
  • VAT — tax setup and returns
  • Accounting — chart of accounts, journal entries
  • Reports — financial and sales reports

Click Open Docs to open the full documentation site at docs.fateherp.com.

Saving Your Settings

After making changes on any tab (except Categories and Docs which save per action), click the Save Settings button in the top-right corner or at the bottom of the tab.

Auto-save: Settings are cached in your browser so they load instantly on next visit. They are also saved to the server so all users on the same company see the same settings.
