Open Settings

Settings

Configure your company profile, invoice defaults, print templates, POS receipts, and item categories — all from one place. Only admin users can access Settings.

To open Settings, click Settings in the left sidebar under the System section.

Settings overview

The Settings page has six tabs across the top. Click any tab to switch between sections:

Company tab

The Company tab controls the details that print on every invoice, quotation, and receipt.

Company Information

Fill in your legal business details. These appear on every printed document and in the ZATCA QR code:

1

Company Name

Your full legal name as registered with ZATCA and CR (e.g. Al Noor Trading Co. Ltd.). This appears in the invoice header.

2

VAT Registration No.

Your 15-digit ZATCA VAT number (e.g. 300012345600003). Required to print tax invoices and generate ZATCA QR codes.

3

CR No. (Commercial Registration)

Your 10-digit CR number (e.g. 1010XXXXXXXX). Shown on printed documents for legal reference.

4

Phone & Email

Your business contact details printed on invoices so customers can reach you easily.

5

Address

Street, city, and country. Printed in the invoice header. Required for ZATCA Phase 2 compliance.

6

Default Currency

Choose SAR, AED, KWD, or another GCC currency. This is pre-filled on all new invoices. Note: the change takes effect from your next login.

Logo & Branding

Upload your company logo to print on invoices, quotations, and orders:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Logo & Branding — drag and drop or click to upload, then choose the print size
1

Click the upload area

Drag your logo file onto the dashed box or click to browse. Accepted formats: PNG, SVG, or JPEG. Keep the file under 800 KB for fast rendering on print.

2

Choose Logo Size on Print

Select Small (40 px), Medium (65 px), or Large (90 px) to control how large the logo appears on printed documents.

3

Click Save Settings

The blue Save Settings button is in the top-right corner of the page. Your logo will appear on the next document you print.

Invoice & Stock tab

Controls how your invoices interact with inventory and what happens when stock runs low.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Invoice & Stock — control how invoices affect your inventory
SettingWhat it doesRecommended for
Update Stock on Invoice When ON, saving a Sales Invoice automatically deducts items from your warehouse stock. When OFF, you must create a separate Delivery Note to move stock. Retail & service businesses — turn ON
Default Warehouse The warehouse items are deducted from when creating invoices. You can still override this on each individual invoice. Set to your main store or stockroom
Allow Negative Stock When ON, you can invoice an item even if the stock balance would go below zero. When OFF, Fateh will block the invoice. Turn OFF for strict stock control; ON for service businesses
VAT Account Setup: This tab has a "Setup VAT Accounts" button. Click it once when you first set up Fateh — it auto-creates the input and output tax accounts in your Chart of Accounts. You only need to do this once.

Print Templates tab

POS Receipt tab

Set up the header, footer, and paper width for receipts printed at the Point of Sale counter.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
POS Receipt — customise every receipt your cashier prints
1

Receipt Header

Text printed at the top of every receipt — use it for your shop name, branch, or a welcome message. Example: "Welcome to Fateh Store — Al Riyadh".

2

Receipt Footer

Text at the bottom — return policy, WhatsApp number, Wi-Fi code. Example: "No refunds after 7 days. WhatsApp: +966 5X XXX XXXX".

3

Paper Width

Choose 80mm for a standard thermal till roll, or A4 for a regular inkjet or laser printer.

4

Show VAT Breakdown

When enabled, the receipt prints a separate line showing the VAT amount. Required for tax invoices above SAR 1,000.

5

Preview & Save

Click Preview Receipt to see how it will look on your printer, then click Save Settings.

Categories tab

Item categories are groups you assign to products and services. On the POS screen they appear as filter buttons, making it faster for cashiers to find items during a sale.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Categories tab — add item groups that will appear as filter buttons in the POS screen

Creating a category

1

Type the category name

Enter a short, clear name in the input at the top — e.g. Electronics, Food & Beverages, Services, Accessories.

2

Click Add

The category appears instantly in the list below and becomes available as an item group in the Items module.

3

Assign items to the category

Go to Stock → Items, open each item, and set its Item Group to the new category. Those items will then appear under that filter in POS.

Deleting a category: Click the trash icon next to a category. If items are still assigned to it, Fateh will block the deletion and show how many items are affected — reassign those items to another group first.

Docs tab

The Docs tab is a built-in shortcut panel to this documentation site, so you can look things up without leaving the app.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Docs tab — click any card to open that section of the documentation in a new tab

Each card opens a different documentation section: Getting Started, Items, Sales, Purchasing, ZATCA, VAT, Accounting, and Reports. The Open Docs button at the bottom opens the full site at docs.fateherp.com.

Saving your settings

After making changes on any tab, click the Save Settings button in the top-right corner of the page.

  • Categories save immediately when you click Add or the trash icon — no Save button needed.
  • Currency change takes effect from your next login.
  • Settings are shared across your whole company — all users on your account will see the updated details.
