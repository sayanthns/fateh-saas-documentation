Reports

Fateh ERP includes a comprehensive set of financial and operational reports.

Accessing Reports

Go to Accounts → Reports (or Selling/Buying → Reports for sales-specific reports). Each report has filters for date range, company, and other parameters.

Key Financial Reports

ReportWhat it showsLocation
Trial BalanceAll account balances for a periodAccounts
Profit & LossRevenue, expenses, and net profitAccounts
Balance SheetAssets, liabilities, and equityAccounts
Accounts ReceivableOutstanding invoices by customer and agingAccounts
Accounts PayableOutstanding bills by supplier and agingAccounts
General LedgerAll transactions for any accountAccounts

Key Sales Reports

ReportWhat it shows
Sales AnalyticsSales by customer, item, or time period
Sales Invoice TrendsMonthly invoice totals with charts
Delivery Note TrendsDelivery volumes over time
Item-wise Sales HistoryHow much of each item was sold
Customer-wise Sales HistoryHow much each customer bought

Exporting Reports

Every report can be exported to:

  • Excel (.xlsx) — for further analysis in spreadsheets
  • CSV — for import into other systems
  • PDF — for sharing or printing

Click the Export button at the top-right of any report.
