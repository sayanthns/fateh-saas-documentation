Stock

Track inventory levels, receive goods into your warehouse, and move stock between locations.

Opening Stock Entry

To record your initial inventory when starting with Fateh ERP:

1

Go to Stock → Stock Entry → New

2

Set Purpose to "Material Receipt"

3

Set Target Warehouse

Select the warehouse where the stock will be stored (e.g., Main Warehouse).

4

Add items and quantities

Add each item with the opening quantity and valuation rate (cost price).

5

Submit

Checking Stock Levels

Go to Stock → Stock Balance or open any Item and go to the Dashboard tab. You'll see the quantity on hand in each warehouse, reserved quantity, and projected quantity.

Stock Entry Types

TypeUse case
Material ReceiptReceiving goods into a warehouse (without a purchase invoice)
Material IssueConsuming goods from stock (e.g., for manufacturing)
Material TransferMoving stock between warehouses
Stock ReconciliationAdjusting stock after a physical count
