Stock & Warehouses

Track inventory levels, warehouse locations, and stock movements in real time.

Deliveries list

Go to Stock → Delivery Note to see all outbound shipments:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Deliveries list — all goods dispatched to customers

Stock Balance

Go to Reports → Stock Balance to see current stock levels:

  • Item — the product name and code
  • Warehouse — which location holds this stock
  • Opening Qty — stock at the start of the period
  • In Qty — stock received (purchase receipts, production)
  • Out Qty — stock dispatched (deliveries, sales)
  • Balance Qty — current on-hand quantity

Warehouses

Fateh supports multiple warehouses. Go to Stock → Warehouse to manage them. For each warehouse you can:

  • Set a warehouse address
  • Assign a default account for stock valuation
  • Create child warehouses for zones or aisles

Stock Entry (Adjustments)

To manually adjust stock levels (physical stock count corrections):

1

Go to Stock → Stock Entry

Click + New Stock Entry.

2

Purpose: Material Receipt

To add stock (e.g., opening balance). Choose Material Receipt.

3

Purpose: Material Issue

To reduce stock (e.g., damaged goods). Choose Material Issue.

4

Add items

Specify the item, warehouse, and quantity.

5

Submit

The stock ledger is updated immediately.
