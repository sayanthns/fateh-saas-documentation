Navigation

Learn how to move around Fateh ERP quickly.

Your Dashboard

The Dashboard is the home screen. It shows you:

  • Today's Sales — total sales invoiced today
  • Today's Collection — payments received today
  • Total Outstanding — money customers still owe you
  • Total Customers — number of customers in your account
yourcompany.fateherp.com
Dashboard overview showing key business metrics

Left sidebar

Click any item in the left menu to open that section. The current section is highlighted in blue. The menu is split into:

  • SALES — for selling to your customers
  • PURCHASE — for buying from your suppliers
  • ACCOUNTING — for financial entries and reports

Opening and closing the sidebar

On desktop, click the grid icon (☰) at the top of the sidebar to collapse it and get more screen space. Click it again to expand.

On mobile, tap the menu icon at the top-left to open the sidebar. Tap anywhere outside it to close.
