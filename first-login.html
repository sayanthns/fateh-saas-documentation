First Login

When your Fateh ERP account is ready, you'll receive a welcome email with your login link. Here's how to sign in for the first time.

Step 1 — Open the login page

Click the link in your welcome email, or go directly to yourcompany.fateherp.com in your browser.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
The Fateh ERP login screen

Step 2 — Enter your email and password

Type the email address and password from your welcome email, then click Sign in.

First time logging in? Your temporary password is in the welcome email. You can change it anytime from your profile settings.

Step 3 — You're in!

After signing in you'll land on the Dashboard, which shows your business at a glance.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
Your Dashboard after signing in — shows today's sales, collections, and totals

The left menu

Everything you need is in the sidebar on the left:

SectionWhat's inside
SalesDashboard, Invoices, POS, Orders, Quotations, Customers, Payments, Items
PurchasePurchase Invoice, Suppliers, Supplier Payments
AccountingJournal Entry, Chart of Accounts, Expense Entry

Forgot your password?

On the login screen, click Forgot password? and enter your email. You'll receive a reset link within a few minutes. Check your spam folder if you don't see it.
