First Login

When your Fateh ERP account is activated, you'll receive a welcome email with your login URL, username, and a temporary password. Follow these steps to get started.

Logging In

1

Open your browser

Go to your unique Fateh ERP URL: yourcompany.fateherp.com. You will see the Fateh login page.

2

Enter your credentials

Type your email address and the temporary password from the welcome email. Click Login.

3

Change your password

On first login, Fateh will ask you to set a new password. Choose a strong password (at least 8 characters, including a number and a symbol). Click Update Password.

4

You're in!

You'll land on the Fateh home workspace showing your modules: Selling, Buying, Accounting, Stock, and more.

Your Profile & Settings

Click your name/avatar in the top-right corner to access:

  • My Profile — update your name, phone number, and profile photo
  • My Settings — change language, date/number format, time zone
  • Change Password — update your login password at any time

Recommended: Set Your Time Zone

Go to My Settings → Time Zone and set it to Asia/Riyadh. This ensures all dates and times on invoices and reports match your local time.

If your time zone is wrong, invoice dates may appear incorrect when submitted to ZATCA.

Forgot Password?

On the login page, click Forgot Password. Enter your email address and click Send Reset Link. Check your inbox (and spam folder) for the password reset email. The link expires in 24 hours.
