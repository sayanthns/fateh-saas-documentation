Customers

Add the companies and individuals you sell to. You need to add a customer before you can create an invoice or quotation for them.

Viewing your customers

Go to Customers in the left menu. You'll see a list of all your customers with their type, VAT number, and outstanding balance.

yourcompany.fateherp.com
The Customers list — all your customers in one place

Adding a new customer

Click the + New Customer button at the top right. A form appears:

yourcompany.fateherp.com
New Customer form — fill in name, type, and VAT number
1

Customer Name

Enter the full name of the company or person. Example: Al Noor Trading Co.

2

Type

Choose Company if you're selling to a business, or Individual for a person.

3

VAT Registration No.

If this customer is VAT-registered in Saudi Arabia, enter their 15-digit VAT number here. This is required for B2B invoices submitted to ZATCA.

4

Click Create Customer

The customer is saved and ready to use in invoices and quotations.

Outstanding balance

The Outstanding column shows how much each customer still owes you (total unpaid invoices). When you record a payment, this number goes down automatically.

You can also add a new customer on-the-fly when creating an invoice — just click the + button next to the Customer field.
