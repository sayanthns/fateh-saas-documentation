Customers

Add and manage your customers. Each customer holds contact details, VAT number, and outstanding balance history.

Adding a New Customer

1

Open Customers

Go to Selling → Customers, then click New.

2

Fill in details

Enter the Customer Name, Customer Type (Company or Individual), and Customer Group. For B2B customers, add the VAT registration number.

3

Add contact & address

In the Contacts section, click New Contact. Add the contact person's name, phone, and email. In the Addresses section, add the billing address (including country).

4

Save

Click Save. The customer is now available in all sales forms.

Viewing Outstanding Balance

Open any Customer and scroll to the Accounts section. You'll see the Outstanding Amount — the total unpaid invoices for this customer. Click it to see a detailed breakdown.

Importing Customers

To import multiple customers at once, go to Settings → Data Import → Import Customer. Download the CSV template, fill in your customers, and upload it. Fateh will validate and import all rows.
