VAT

Fateh ERP handles Saudi Arabia's 15% VAT automatically. Understand how VAT is applied and how to file your VAT return.

How VAT Works in Fateh ERP

When you add an item to a Sales Invoice with a VAT 15% tax template, Fateh automatically:

  • Calculates 15% of the net amount
  • Adds it to the invoice total
  • Posts the VAT amount to the Output VAT ledger
  • Includes the VAT breakdown in the ZATCA XML

VAT Categories

CategoryRateExample
Standard Rate15%Most goods and services
Zero-Rated0%Exports, international transport, certain medicines
Exempt—Residential real estate, certain financial services

Preparing Your VAT Return

1

Go to Accounts → VAT Return

Select the VAT return period (quarterly or monthly, depending on your ZATCA filing frequency).

2

Review output VAT

This is the VAT you collected on all your Sales Invoices during the period.

3

Review input VAT

This is the VAT you paid on your Purchase Invoices — the amount you can deduct.

4

Net VAT payable = Output VAT − Input VAT

If positive, you owe this to ZATCA. If negative, you have a VAT credit.

Fateh ERP generates the VAT Return report but you still need to log into the ZATCA portal to submit and pay the return manually (or through your accountant).
