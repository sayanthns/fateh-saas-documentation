Purchase Invoices

Record bills you receive from suppliers. This tracks what you owe and lets you claim back the VAT you paid.

Recording a purchase invoice

Click + New Purchase Invoice and fill in the details from the supplier's bill:

1

Select the supplier

Pick the supplier from the list. If they're new, add them in the Suppliers section first.

2

Enter the supplier's invoice number

This is the reference number printed on the paper invoice you received from the supplier.

3

Set the invoice date

Use the date shown on the supplier's invoice, not today's date.

4

Add items and amounts

Enter what you purchased and the amounts from the bill. Tick Apply VAT if the supplier charged VAT — this gets recorded as input VAT you can claim back.

5

Save

The amount is now shown as payable to the supplier.

The VAT you record on purchase invoices reduces the VAT you owe to ZATCA. Always enter supplier invoices with the correct VAT number.
