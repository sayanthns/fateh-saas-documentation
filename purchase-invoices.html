Purchase Invoices

Record bills from your suppliers, claim input VAT, and keep track of what you owe.

Creating a Purchase Invoice

1

Go to Buying → Purchase Invoice → New

2

Select Supplier

Type and select the supplier name. If the supplier has default payment terms, they'll be applied automatically.

3

Enter supplier invoice details

In the Supplier Invoice No. field, enter the reference number from the supplier's invoice. Set the Supplier Invoice Date to match their document.

4

Add items

Add the items/services purchased with quantities and rates. Select the correct VAT template to claim input VAT.

5

Submit

Click Submit. The amount is added to the supplier's outstanding payable balance.

Input VAT Claim

When you add a VAT tax template to a purchase invoice, Fateh automatically posts the input VAT to your Input Tax Credit account. This amount can be offset against your output VAT when you file your VAT return.

You can only claim input VAT on invoices from VAT-registered suppliers. Always verify the supplier's VAT number before claiming.
