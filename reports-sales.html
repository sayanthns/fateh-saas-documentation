Sales Reports

Analyze your sales performance, top customers, and best-selling items.

Sales Analytics

Go to Selling → Reports → Sales Analytics. This is the most powerful sales report in Fateh ERP. You can:

  • Group by Customer, Item, Territory, or Sales Person
  • View by Monthly, Quarterly, or Yearly
  • Compare periods side-by-side

Sales Invoice Trends

Go to Accounts → Reports → Sales Invoice Trends. Shows total invoiced amount per month with a trend chart. Useful for spotting seasonal patterns.

Customer-wise Sales History

Go to Selling → Reports → Customer-wise Sales History. See all invoices for each customer with totals. Use this to identify your top customers and track their buying behaviour over time.

Item-wise Sales History

Go to Selling → Reports → Item-wise Sales History. See how many units of each item you've sold and the total revenue per item.
